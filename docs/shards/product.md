# Product Shard

## Purpose

Keep FlowAI focused as a Business-to-Workflow Chatbot Generator.

## Scope

Business inputs, business understanding, workflow draft, validation, testing, preview, export.

## Current Decisions

- Business understanding comes before visual editing.
- Exportability is core product value.
- Avoid generic builder clone behavior.

## Do Not Do

- Do not turn FlowAI into a memory system.
- Do not build a prompt-only wrapper.
- Do not copy Dify/Flowise/Typebot positioning.
