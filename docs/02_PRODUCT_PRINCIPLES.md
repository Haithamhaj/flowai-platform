# 02 Product Principles

- Business understanding before visual editing.
- Generated workflow must be editable.
- Safe runtime before AI magic.
- Testability before channels.
- Channel adapters are replaceable.
- Exportability is a product feature.
- Avoid cloning Dify, Flowise, Typebot, or any generic builder.
- Calm operational UI later, not decorative UI.
- Prefer simple user-facing flows and modular internal boundaries.
- Make uncertain assumptions visible to the user.
